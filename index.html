Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about starting your author journey.

Who is Inkscribers for?
Inkscribers is designed for first-time and early-stage authors who are serious about completing and publishing their book with structured guidance.
Do you write the book on behalf of the author?
No. Inkscribers does not provide ghostwriting services. The work focuses on mentorship, structure, preparation, and guidance while the author retains full ownership of the writing.
What stage should my manuscript be at?
Authors can approach Inkscribers at any stage — idea, partial draft, or completed manuscript. The support offered depends on the current stage of the work.
How long does the process usually take?
Timelines vary based on the manuscript stage and scope of support. Clear timelines are discussed and defined before any engagement begins.
Is publication guaranteed?
No guarantees are made regarding sales, rankings, or outcomes. The focus is on preparing the manuscript and author to meet professional publishing standards.
What platforms do you support for publishing?
Support is provided for common online publishing platforms. Platform selection depends on the author’s goals and publishing plan.
What is included in the free author consultation?
The consultation is used to understand the author’s stage, goals, and requirements, and to determine whether Inkscribers is the right fit for the project.
Do you provide marketing or promotion services?
Inkscribers does not offer active marketing or promotional campaigns. Guidance related to presentation and positioning may be provided as part of selected packages.
How is the scope of work defined?
All engagements operate within a clearly defined scope and timeline, agreed upon before the work begins.
How do I get started?
The first step is to book an author consultation using the form on this website.
